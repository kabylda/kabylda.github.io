I am a Ph.D. researcher in the Theoretical Chemical Physics group of Prof. Alexandre Tkatchenko at the University of Luxembourg. Currently focused on extending the applicability of machine learning force fields to larger molecules by accurately describing long-range interactions.

I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Chemistry with focus in Physical/Quantum Chemistry at the Lomonosov Moscow State University in 2021. During this time, I did research in organic, laser and quantum chemistry.

Besides chemistry, my interests include cs:go, chess, basketball, and techno.
